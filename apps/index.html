Why EyeSaver?

In this digital age, our eyes are constantly exposed to screens, leading to digital eye strain and fatigue. EyeSaver is your solution to promote healthier screen habits and protect your eyes from the harmful effects of prolonged screen time.

Key Features:

  • Free!
  • Regular Breaks: Blanks your screens every 20 minutes to give you a 20 second break to reduce eye fatigue.
  • Gentle: Will not interrupt you when you are actively typing on your keyboard.
  • Online aware: Will not interrupt you when you are using your camera or microphone.

Note: EyeSaver is compatible with Windows operating systems.
